寶貝學習樂園・隱私權政策

Privacy Policy  |  生效日 Effective: 2026-06-26
完全離線・不收集任何資料

寶貝學習樂園（以下稱「本 App」）非常重視兒童與家長的隱私。本 App 完全離線運作，不會收集、儲存或分享任何個人資料。

“Kids Learning Park” ("the App") respects the privacy of children and parents. The App works entirely offline and does not collect, store, or share any personal data.

1. 我們不收集的資料Information we do NOT collect

本 App 不會收集下列任何資料：

  • 姓名、電子郵件、電話或任何聯絡資訊
  • 位置、相片、麥克風、聯絡人
  • 裝置識別碼、廣告 ID
  • 使用行為、分析或追蹤資料

The App does not collect names, email, phone numbers, location, photos, microphone input, contacts, device identifiers, advertising IDs, or any usage/analytics data.

2. 本機儲存的資料Data stored only on your device

學習進度、貼紙、獎盃與解鎖狀態僅儲存在你裝置的本機空間，不會傳出裝置、也不會上傳到任何伺服器。移除本 App 即會一併清除這些資料。

Learning progress, stickers, trophies, and unlock status are saved only in your device's local storage. This data never leaves the device and is removed when you uninstall the App.

3. 網路與權限Network & permissions

本 App 不要求網路權限，不會連線、不會上傳任何資料。語音與音效皆內建於 App 中，於裝置本機播放。

The App requests no network permission and makes no internet connection. All voice and sound are bundled in the App and played locally.

4. 沒有第三方服務No third-party services

本 App 不含廣告、不含第三方分析、不含社群登入，也不會與任何第三方分享資料。

The App contains no ads, no third-party analytics, and no social logins. No data is shared with any third party.

5. 兒童隱私Children's privacy

本 App 專為幼兒設計。由於我們完全不收集任何個人資料，因此符合美國《兒童線上隱私保護法》(COPPA) 及相關兒童隱私規範的要求。我們絕不會向兒童索取個人資訊。

The App is designed for young children. Because we collect no personal data at all, the App complies with COPPA and applicable children's privacy regulations. We never request personal information from children.

6. 付費解鎖In-app purchase

若本 App 提供付費解鎖功能，交易一律由 Google Play 處理。我們不會接觸、收集或儲存你的付款資訊。付款相關處理適用 Google Play 的隱私政策。

If the App offers a paid unlock, the transaction is handled entirely by Google Play. We never access, collect, or store your payment information. Google's handling is governed by the Google Play privacy policy.

7. 政策變更Changes to this policy

若本政策有任何更新，我們會修改本頁面內容並更新上方的「生效日」。

If this policy is updated, we will revise this page and update the "Effective" date above.
